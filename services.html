Lingkhar Lodge

The Lingkhar Lodge warmly invites you to immerse yourself in a captivating experience surrounded by stunning scenery and hospitable services. Nestled in a serene countryside near Trashigang, Eastern Bhutan, this lodge ensures a tranquil and enjoyable stay, boasting elegant accommodations, a diverse restaurant menu, conference facilities, and traditional hot stone bath amenities for ultimate relaxation.

Druk Deothjung Resort

Located in the quaint town of Trashigang in eastern Bhutan, we have crafted a luxurious hotel amidst stunning natural surroundings, allowing guests to revel in both beauty and comfort. With breathtaking panoramic views spanning from Trashigang Dzong to Kanglung valley and the Drangme Chhu River, Druk Deothjung Resort offers a blend of elegance and Bhutanese cultural charm.
